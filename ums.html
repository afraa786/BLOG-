Student Information Dashboard

Student Leave

Student Leave: Students are allowed to take a specific number of leaves each semester based on university guidelines. Generally, students must apply for leave through the university's leave management system, providing necessary documentation such as medical certificates or personal reasons. To be eligible to sit for exams, students must not exceed the maximum allowed leave days. Excessive leave may require additional approval from academic advisors or deans. It is essential for students to keep track of their leave days to ensure they meet attendance requirements and avoid any academic penalties.

Student Attendance

Student Grades: The grading system evaluates student performance based on various assessments including exams, assignments, and class participation. Grades are assigned according to predefined criteria and are crucial for determining academic standing and eligibility for graduation. Students can view their grades through the university's online portal, which provides detailed feedback on their performance. Regular monitoring of grades is advised to track progress and address any academic issues promptly. Additionally, students can request grade reviews or discuss their performance with instructors if needed.

Student Fees

Student Fees: Tuition fees and other related charges are a significant part of university education. Students are required to pay fees by specified deadlines to ensure uninterrupted access to academic resources and facilities. The fee structure includes tuition, lab fees, library fees, and other miscellaneous charges. Students can view their fee statements and payment history through the university's financial management system. It is important to make timely payments and manage finances to avoid any late fees or holds on academic records. Financial aid and scholarships may be available to assist with the cost of education.

Student feebdback forms

Providing feedback on faculty members is a crucial aspect of maintaining a high-quality academic environment and ensuring effective teaching and learning experiences. With this, students have the opportunity to share their thoughts and evaluations regarding their interactions with faculty, including teaching effectiveness, clarity of communication, and availability for support. Constructive feedback allows students to voice their opinions and suggestions on how faculty members can improve their teaching methods and engagement.

Faculty Information Dashboard

Faculty Leave

Faculty Leave: Faculty members are entitled to take various types of leave as per university policies. These may include personal leave, medical leave, and professional development leave. To apply for leave, faculty members must submit a formal request along with supporting documents, such as medical certificates or justification for personal reasons, to their department head or HR department. Effective leave management is essential for maintaining academic schedules and ensuring that departmental functions continue smoothly in the absence of a faculty member. The university's leave management system helps track and manage leave requests efficiently.

Faculty Attendance

Faculty Attendance: Maintaining accurate attendance records is crucial for both administrative purposes and compliance with university policies. Faculty members are required to record their attendance in the university's attendance management system or manually as per departmental guidelines. Regular monitoring of attendance helps in managing workloads, tracking faculty presence, and ensuring that all scheduled classes and meetings are conducted as planned. Any discrepancies or issues should be reported to the administration promptly.

Faculty Development

Faculty Development: Ongoing professional development is vital for faculty to stay current with educational trends, research advancements, and teaching methodologies. The university offers various development programs, including workshops, seminars, and training sessions, to support faculty growth. Participation in these programs not only enhances teaching effectiveness but also contributes to personal and professional growth. Faculty members are encouraged to engage in development activities to continuously improve their skills and knowledge.

Faculty Feedback

Faculty Feedback: Providing and receiving feedback is an integral part of fostering a positive and effective academic environment. This allows faculty members to submit their feedback on various aspects of university operations, including administrative processes, teaching resources, and support services. Feedback is reviewed regularly to identify areas for improvement and to implement changes that enhance the overall working experience. Constructive feedback helps in building a collaborative and supportive academic community.

Faculty Contact Information

Faculty Contact Information: The essential contact details of faculty members, including their email addresses, phone numbers, and office hours. Having accessible contact information ensures that students, colleagues, and administrative staff can reach out for academic inquiries, scheduling meetings, or seeking assistance. Keeping contact details up-to-date is important for effective communication and fostering strong professional relationships within the university.

Faculty Research and Publications

Faculty Research and Publications: Showcasing research and scholarly work is important for recognizing faculty contributions to the academic community. This highlights ongoing research projects, published articles, conference presentations, and other scholarly activities undertaken by faculty members. It serves as a platform to celebrate academic achievements, encourage collaboration, and promote research excellence. Displaying research accomplishments not only enhances the reputation of the faculty but also contributes to the university's overall research profile.
